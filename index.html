Welcome Aboard!

Come join the crew of Tiki Time on an unforgettable 2 hour tour of beautiful Long Lake. Just a short distance from downtown Traverse City, MI. Relax on the pristine waters worry free with one of our USCG licensed captains guiding your tour with stunning views of the surrounding countryside.

Snacks and beverages of your choice are always welcome. Visit our friends at Long Lake Grocery for all your needs.
